# Sankofa Kitchen African Food Word Puzzle

An interactive word search game that celebrates African foodways through language and play. Discover the rich culinary heritage of Africa one word at a time.

## Play the Game

**[Play the live game here](https://chicafricanculture.github.io/african-food-word-puzzle/)**

## About the Game

"Taste the Words of Africa" is more than just a word puzzle—it's a journey through the diverse culinary landscape of the African continent. As you search for food-related words, you'll unlock cultural facts and traditional recipes that bring each ingredient and dish to life.

**Before you cook it, learn to say it. These are the words of African foodways.**

### Features

- Interactive word search grid featuring African food terms
- Light/Dark mode toggle for comfortable playing
- Hint system to help when you're stuck
- Immersive audio: traditional drum beats and ambient desert wind
- Victory animation with cultural celebration
- Cultural facts revealed as words are found
- Fully responsive design for all devices

## How to Play

1. **Find Words**: Look at the word list and find each word hidden in the grid
2. **Click and Drag**: Click on the first letter and drag to the last letter of a word
3. **Discover Facts**: Each found word reveals cultural information and sometimes a recipe
4. **Use Hints**: Click the 💡 Hint button if you need help
5. **Complete the Circle**: Find all words to unlock the victory screen
